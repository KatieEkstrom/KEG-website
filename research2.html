Overview

I work at the intersection of cognitive science and communication sciences, examining how people co-construct stories and common ground in conversation. My approach blends experimental, corpus, and systems perspectives to reveal how meaning is coordinated in real time. Conversational structure, narrative exchange, and how shared meaning emerges across cognitive & communication sciences.
